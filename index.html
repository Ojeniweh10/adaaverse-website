Our Mission

Advancing Development in Africa for Africans(ADAFA) is a driver of change in Africa along side ADAAVERSE which extends developmental effect globally..

We are focused on six major aspects: Governance, Funding, Education, Water, Climate Action, and Technology as structured by Adafa, Government, NonGovernmental Organizations, and Companies. The organization was necessitated due to the social exclusion of nearly 95% of Africans with few, adequately educated, have access to clean water, opportued to exercise their human rights. We endorse companies, organizations and individuals business (s) in Africa, that are investing some percentage of business earnings to humanitarian activities hence promoting societal development in its host country or continent. This will solve some of the social and economic challenges people face locally and globally. Adafa have its operational headquarters in Nigeria. Organizations and companies in the international communities that support the black community and Africa at large will be endorsed by adaaverse, adaaverse covers businesses in the whole universe. Endorsement from ADAFA or ADAAVERSE is aimed at promoting the businesses of companies, organizations and agencies, after complete verification has been made to ascertain the credibility of the organisation.

GOVERNANCE

Transparency, accountability and responsive governance in Africa plays a crucial role in unlocking the continent’s potential for investment and sustainable growth. Effective governance and a strong social contract are critical to the successful implementation of the ADAFA strategy in creating an enabling business environment and social inclusion which can facilitate trade, promote economic development and social stability in Africa
